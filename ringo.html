Siberians

Ringo is a Siberian cat, this is a breed that comes from Russia. They are one of the largest cat breeds only following MainCoons. Due to Ringo bring a mix he is not as large as they come but is certaly one of the longest cats I have seen. Siberians have curled claws aposed to the sight ones that are seen on common domestic cats. They aslo have extra fur on there feet to help them make there way threw the snow. Siberians are often very warm cats who once you gain the trust of would love nothing more then to curl up next to you.
